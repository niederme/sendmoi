SendMoi

Your inbox, in two taps.

SendMoi sends links to Gmail as rich email cards, so they're easy to find and act on later.

Share setup

Set up SendMoi in the Share Sheet

Do this once, and SendMoi is always available when you share a link.

1. Open Share and tap More

From the first app row in the share sheet, open More to edit your app list.

2. Add SendMoi to Favorites

Tap the green plus next to SendMoi so it appears in Favorites.

3. Keep SendMoi enabled

Make sure SendMoi stays toggled on, then tap Done.

Your Gmail, kept private

SendMoi uses your own Gmail account and stays local-first, with no backend storing your session, recipients, or queued sends.

Readable

Readable when it lands

The result is easy to scan: a clean subject, useful context, and a source link that still feels good on both phone and desktop.

Offline queue

If the network is flaky, the send is not dropped. SendMoi keeps it in a local queue and retries when the app wakes up or connectivity returns.

Recent recipients

Keep a default recipient ready, then switch fast with recent-recipient shortcuts when something needs to go somewhere else.
